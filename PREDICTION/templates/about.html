About Us

Welcome to our Medical Drug Sales Prediction platform, an innovative solution aimed at revolutionizing inventory management for pharmacists and drug sellers in Sri Lanka.

Our mission is to empower the pharmaceutical industry with accurate, data-driven insights that enable better decision-making and more efficient stock management. Through advanced data analysis and machine learning techniques, our platform helps predict drug sales, ensuring optimal stock levels, reducing wastage, and improving the availability of essential medicines.

We are a dedicated team of data scientists, software developers, and industry experts committed to creating impactful technological solutions that address real-world challenges. Our vision is to extend the capabilities of our platform to offer comprehensive pharmacy management tools that contribute to a more resilient healthcare system in Sri Lanka and beyond.
